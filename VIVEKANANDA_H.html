Swami Vivekananda (1863-1902) is famous for introducing Hinduism to the West, spreading Vedanta and Yoga, and inspiring the Indian independence movement.

I am expressing my sweet memory and also that is my achievement.....................

When I was in 9th standard, I participated in an event. There was a Samiji in front of our house. He conducted an event on the day of Swami Vivekananda's birth anniversary.They have a trust. The name of that trust is Sarada Trust. They give scholarships to the poor. I am also eligible for that scholarship. In that event, I played the role of Sarada Mata. We did a skit. Then I also appeared in the paper. I think that was my first success. That Swamiji's name was Archanananda. After we participated in that event, it was in the next day's paper. This is the photo......
